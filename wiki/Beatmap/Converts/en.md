# Converts

![The \"Show converted\" toggle in osu!(lazer)](img/show-converted-button.png "The \"Show converted\" toggle in osu!(lazer)")

In osu! gameplay, **converts** (or *converted beatmaps*) are [beatmaps](/wiki//Beatmap) that are  played in the game mode that it's not made for. These beatmaps are automatically generated based on certain parameters present on the original beatmap.

As it stands, osu! supports conversions of beatmaps from the [osu! game mode](/wiki/Game_mode/osu!) to osu!taiko, osu!catch, and osu!mania.
